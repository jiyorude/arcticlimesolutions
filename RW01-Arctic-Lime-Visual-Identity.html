GRAPHIC DESIGN

Striking, contrastful and modern. Those are just three ways of many to describe the visual identity of ARCTIC LIME AudioVisual. The main goal for its visual design was to be recognizable, bold, and to fully grasp the arctic lime color shade in shape, form and contrast.

The ARCTIC LIME AudioVisual logo contains all of the lime color shades found within the corporate color pallette in order to create a striking visual logo that contains depth. The text in the logo completes

The typeset truly breathes ARCTIC LIME. The bold 'Basement Grotesque font' steals the show for all of our headings, while the Lunchtype and ClearSans fonts are clear, concise, modern and accessible in paragraphs and open text. The dark purple and pink colors are eyecatchers that show a nice contrast with the limegreen shades.

The corporate design found is fresh, accessible, modern and to the point. It contains a mix of playfullness and the to-the-point corporate style while maintaining its sharpness and clarity.
