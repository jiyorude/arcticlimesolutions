WELCOME TO ARCTIC LIME AUDIOVISUAL!


ARCTIC LIME AudioVisual is a digital post-production studio based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. The studio was found in the summer of 2021. Thanks to years of previous experience in the post-production field, ARCTIC LIME AudioVisual is able to assist you with many of your audiovisual needs; such as: video-editing, motion graphics, audio-mixing, color grading and delivery for practically any screen or setup. This is why ARCTIC’s tagline is: Video. For Every Screen. ARCTIC LIME is a highly versatile and flexible studio with an emphasis on digital workflows and delivery. ARCTIC LIME AudioVisual is een 'digital-first' post-productie studio in Amsterdam. De studio is opgericht in de zomer van 2021.
